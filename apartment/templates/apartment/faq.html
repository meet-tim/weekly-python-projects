We have gathered a few of the questions most of our users ask which may be what you are looking for. We have briefly answered all of them. In case you can't find what you are looking for, contact us and send us your question. We will be happy to help.

1.Do you have to pay a fee to register?

Creating an account with us is free. We do not charge any fees or sell you some of our services. We are a group of commited people who want to help society.

  • Please do not send any money to anyone who claims to be us.
  • If you want to support Appartment contact us for our details
  • Make sure you see the house owner in person before you pay any amount.
2.How do i get my house featured?
Every week Appartment selects 3 of the best deals and features them.
3. Why are some houses having the appartment logo as their picture?
The appartment logo is used as a placeholder if the owner of the house has not posted any images of the house.
4. How can I add more images to my house?

Currently Appartment gives out only 5 images per house. We have done this for some reasons..

  • Large online storage capacity is expensive.
  • We want to maintain the speed at which the website responds to users.

We are gradually building the site and making it better so We hope to include it in our future update.

In case you haven't found the answer for your question please feel free to contact us, our customer support will be happy to help you.
